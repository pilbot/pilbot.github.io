Dexit Means Dexit

September 3, 2019
Pokémon National Pokédex Dexit

In the most controversial political move this century the Pokémon company has stated that in the Galar region we no longer “Gotta catch ‘em all"™, in fact we can’t they won’t all be available!

Since the number of Pokémon in Kanto and Dunbar’s number are approximately equal I think that is a fair number of older Pokémon that will make it across the border. I’m assuming in Galar the legendary creatures can get a special visa or something.

Here is my list of the 151 that better be in Sword and Shield if it wants to remain strong and stable, remember folks:
